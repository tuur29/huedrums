As long as you press a drum, the corresponding lamp will turn on (or off depending on how it started).

Drag your finger horizontally across the drum to change the lamp's color, drag it vertically to change it's brightness.

Strobe

When you touch the vast blackness that surrounds the drums and buttons the screen will start strobing.

Dragging your finger horizontally will also change it's color. The color will reset to white on every touch.

Adding a bassline

Adding a bassline is simple! First, enable the 'loop' mode, next tap and hold a drum. The lamp will continue to flicker as long as you hold the drum.

   Customizing the drums

You can customize how the drums look by either tapping the move or resize buttons at the top.

To resize a drum, hold it and drag vertically.

More features

 Permanently toggle the state of a lamp so it flickers off instead of on.

 Lock all colors so you don't accidentally change your setup mid-drumming.

 You can ease the flickering of your drums in the settings for a more relaxed lightshow.

 Hide certain 'boring' or unwanted drums.
