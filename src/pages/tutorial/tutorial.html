Drumming

As long as you press a drum, the corresponding lamp will turn on (or off depending on how it started).

Drag your finger horizontally across the drum to change the lamp's color, drag it vertically to change it's brightness.

Strobing

When you hold the vast blackness that surrounds the drums and buttons the screen will start strobing.

Dragging your finger horizontally will also change it's color. The color will reset to white on every touch.

Looping

Adding a bassline is simple! First, press the 'loop' button, next tap and hold a drum. The lamp will continue to flicker as long as you hold the drum.

   Customizing

You can customize how the drums look by either tapping the 'move' or 'resize' buttons.

To resize a drum, hold it and drag vertically.

Tip: Hold and drag the move icon
to move the button panel around.
